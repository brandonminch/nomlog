# Ideas to iterate on

Uncommitted ideas, experiments, and "maybe someday" items. No need for full ADRs here—use this as a backlog to revisit and promote to the [roadmap](roadmap.md) or to a [decision](decisions/) when appropriate.

## Product

- (e.g. Meal templates / favorites, quick-add from recent meals, barcode scanning.)
- Future meal logging: allow logging meals in advance and receive a notification at the pre-logged meal time to verify that’s what you ate; after verification, it counts towards your daily nutrition.
- Shopping list generation: generate a grocery list from a saved weekly meal plan.
- Fasting support: mark meals as skipped for fasting windows and receive notifications or reminders that respect fasting schedules.
- Activity logging: log workouts and show net calories for the day by combining exercise burn with logged intake. (UI entry point already exists in the home screen and chat; backend logging not yet implemented.)
- Photo of meal receipt
- Q&A-style nutrition coach: ask questions about nutrition and history to surface gaps or personalized suggestions to improve nutrition.
- Nutrition alerts on meals: show warnings or helpful callouts on logged meals (e.g. high in sodium, great source of fiber, unusually low in protein).
- Workout plans: plan out multi-week workouts, set fitness goals, and tie progress back to nutrition and weight trends.

## Experience

- (e.g. Onboarding flow, help/docs, nutrition insights beyond weekly stats.)
- Log with Siri
- Meal detail page insights: highlight macro balance vs target, biggest nutrient contributors, “what to add next” suggestions (e.g. add fiber/protein), and notable callouts (high sodium/saturated fat, low protein) with quick actions.
- Meal detail comparisons: compare this meal to your typical breakfast/lunch/dinner (macros, calories, nutrients) and show a “better/worse than usual” summary.
- Ingredient/food-item insights: surface which logged items drive calories/macros most, and suggest simple swaps (e.g. leaner protein, lower-sugar drink) to hit targets.

- Scope user recipes so each user only sees and queries their own recipes by default.
